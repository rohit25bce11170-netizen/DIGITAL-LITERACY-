Stay Safe Online Checklist
General Safety

Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication.

Keep your devices and apps updated to patch security vulnerabilities.

Avoid clicking on suspicious links or attachments in emails, messages, or social media.

Log out of shared or public computers after use.

Be cautious about what you share online—limit personal details like address, phone number, or college ID.

Regularly review your social media privacy settings to control who sees your posts.
Financial / UPI Safety

Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking credentials with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank.

Verify payment requests carefully; double-check recipient details before approving any UPI transaction.

Reporting Channels

If you face online fraud, harassment, or cybercrime, report it immediately at the National Cyber Crime Portal:cybercrime.gov.in.

You can also call the Cybercrime Helpline: 1930 for urgent assistance.
