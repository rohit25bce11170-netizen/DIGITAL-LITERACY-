
SOCIAL MEDIA CHECKLIST
✅ Do’s
Think before posting – ensure your content reflects positively on you.

Respect privacy – protect your own information and respect others’ boundaries.

Engage professionally – use social media to build networks and showcase achievements.

Verify information – share only credible and accurate sources.

Balance usage – manage time wisely to avoid distractions from academics.

❌ Don'ts
Don’t overshare personal details – avoid posting sensitive information like addresses or financial data.

Don’t post inappropriate content – steer clear of offensive, discriminatory, or harmful material.

Don’t engage in cyberbullying – never use social media to harass or demean others.

Don’t spread rumors – avoid forwarding unverified or misleading information.

Don’t neglect digital etiquette – avoid excessive tagging, spamming, or unprofessional communication.
